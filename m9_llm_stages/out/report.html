For B1 (assembly rotation), pin_hinge is chosen because it realizes bounded rotation about a fixed axis, as per MECHSYNTH_SPEC_v0.1 §3.3 Card 1 — pin_hinge. For B2 (use-phase rotation), the same pin_hinge is used since it enables repeated lid opening/closing through large angles. The snap_hook_cantilever (E2) is selected for B3, B4, and B5 because it provides a hand-fastenable snap with no added parts, per SNAPFIT_STARTER_v0 §2 and BASF/Bayer Snap-Fit Design Guide p.5 Fig.1. E2 imposes the constraint that the hook must lie outside swept volumes of rotating hosts (D-ONT-12), which is satisfied by its placement on P2 and catch on P1, with E1 providing the rotational axis constraint.
